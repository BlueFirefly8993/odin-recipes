Black Pepper Chicken

Description

This spicy and savoury dish is a perfect companion to a bowl of plain white rice.

Instead of chicken, you can replace it with any kind of protein you want, even tofu to make it vegan.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken thigh or breast
  • Green capsicum
  • Onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Ginger

Sauce

  • Oyster sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken into bite size pieces.
  2. Marinate the chicken with soy sauce, cooking wine, salt, pepper, sesame oil and corn starch.
  3. Cut the vegetables into bite size pieces as well.
  4. Thinly slice the ginger.
  5. Mix the ingredients for sauce.
  6. Heat oil in wok and cook the chicken until golden brown.
  7. Add minced garlic and ginger and stir fry until aromatic.
  8. Add sauce and the remaining vegetables.
  9. Stir fry until the sauce thickens.
  10. Enjoy with some plain white rice!
