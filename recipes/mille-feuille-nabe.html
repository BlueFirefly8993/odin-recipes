Mille Feuille Nabe

Description

A simple and hearty dish to enjoy as the weather is getting colder.

Claypot or nabe pot is preferable, but you can use any pot that you have.

Ingredients

  • Chinese long cabbage
  • Thinly sliced pork belly or pork shoulder (shabu shabu cuts)
  • Sake (Japanese cooking wine)
  • Dashi or dashi powder
  • Water
  • Spring onion (as garnish)

Steps:

  1. Season pork slices with salt and pepper.
  2. Separate the cabbage leaf by leaf.
  3. Place the seasoned pork on top of the cabbage leaf, then stack them together.
  4. Cut the stacked cabbage into three and arrange them in the pot vertically.
  5. Pour sake and dashi into the pot until half filled.
  6. Boil it with medium heat until the pork is cooked.
  7. Garnish with spring onion and serve it with ponzu sauce and Japanese sesame sauce.
